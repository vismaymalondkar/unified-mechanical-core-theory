## 1. The Event Horizon (The Acoustic Waterfall)
Standard physics struggles to explain how a "massless" photon is trapped by a Black Hole. In the UPT, light is a ripple in a $10^{11}$ density superfluid, and the explanation is purely mechanical:

* **The Inflow:** Gravity is the **Bernoulli Sink**—the inward pressure/flow of the universal medium toward a mass. 
* **The Speed Limit:** Light travels through this medium at the absolute acoustic speed limit ($c$).
* **The Sonic Horizon:** The Event Horizon is the exact radius where the inward flow of the universal medium reaches $c$. 
* **The Trap:** If a photon is fired outward from the horizon, it moves at $c$ relative to the fluid, but the fluid is falling inward at $c$. Like a swimmer trying to move 5mph upstream against a 5mph current, the net progress is zero. Inside this radius, the "waterfall" is faster than $c$, sweeping all ripples toward the center regardless of their direction.
