## 2. Light: The Open Corkscrew (Bosons / Spin 1)
When a core drops to a lower harmonic (e.g., during nuclear fission) and releases energy ($E=mc^2$), the excess trapped pressure breaks its tether. The closed knot violently snaps open.

* **The Geometry (Helical Wave):** A photon is not a donut; it is a released, unrolled **Corkscrew**. It retains the rotational momentum of the core that birthed it, traveling forward through the medium as a spinning, helical transverse wave. 
* **Zero Gravity (Massless):** Because the wave is unrolled and traveling at the strict speed limit of the medium ($c$), it does not trap fluid in a localized space. It requires zero resting inward tension to maintain itself. Therefore, a photon exerts exactly **zero gravity** and is entirely massless.
* **The Spin Cycle (360°):** Without the "inside tube" of a Torus to navigate, the open corkscrew simply twists around a single forward-moving axis. One complete rotation of this spiral takes exactly 360°, which perfectly correlates to one single **Wavelength** ($\lambda$). This is why physics categorizes light as "Spin 1."
