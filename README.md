## ⚙️ Core Architecture (The Axioms)

To run this universe, you only need to accept two foundational variables:

1.  **The Absolute Medium:** Space is not empty. It is a physical, high-density ($\rho \approx 10^{11} \text{ kg/m}^3$) acoustic superfluid. It exists under a constant, crushing baseline static pressure ($P_0$).
2.  **The Standard Pipe:** There are no "fundamental particles." Matter is simply the **Standard Universal Pipe**—a fluid vortex tube—tied into various 3D topological knots.

From these two rules, all "forces" emerge as simple plumbing consequences:

* **Mass (Dual-State):** * *Resting:* **Hydrostatic Displacement** ($\Delta V$). The energy of the "hole" in the high-pressure medium.
    * *Inertial:* **Hydrodynamic Drag** ($A_{eff}$). The resistance of the knot's wake in the $10^{11}$ ocean.
* **Charge:** **Volumetric Flow Rate** ($Q$). The amount of fluid the "pump" moves per second.
* **Gravity:** The **Inward Pressure Gradient** (Bernoulli Sink). The suction created by the knot's internal spin.
* **Magnetism:** The **Torsional Wake**. The swirling current created as a "pump" (charge) moves through the medium.
