# 🌌 Unified Mechanical Core Theory (UMCT)

## 📖 Project Abstract
Modern theoretical physics is suffering from severe architectural tech debt. The standard models rely on "wave-particle duality," "spooky action at a distance," and "invisible dark matter" to make their math compile. The logic is inconsistent, fragmented across different scales (Quantum vs. Cosmological), and relies heavily on abstract, non-physical probabilities.

**The Unified Mechanical Core Theory (UMCT)** is a complete refactor of the universe. 

This repository patches the bugs of modern physics by treating the universe as a purely deterministic, local, fluid-mechanical engine. By replacing 0-dimensional point particles and empty space with 3D fluid vortices (Solitons) and a high-tension acoustic medium, the anomalies of both quantum mechanics and general relativity naturally resolve themselves using classical geometry and fluid dynamics.

---

## ⚙️ Core Architecture (The Axioms)
To run this universe, you only need to accept two foundational variables:
1. **The Universal Medium:** Space is not empty. It is a physical, high-tension fluid medium (an acoustic fabric) with a strict wave-propagation speed limit ($c$). 
2. **The Unified Core (The Soliton):** There is only one fundamental particle. Matter is simply a self-sustaining, 3D wave-knot (a Toroidal Vortex) formed out of the universal medium itself.

From these two rules, all fundamental forces (Gravity, Electromagnetism, Nuclear) emerge strictly as mechanical consequences of fluid pressure, viscosity, and torsion. No "magic" required.
