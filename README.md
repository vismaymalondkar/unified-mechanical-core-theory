Modern theoretical physics is suffering from severe architectural tech debt. **The Universal Plumbing Theory (UPT)** is a complete mechanical refactor.

This repository treats the universe as a purely deterministic, local, **fluid-mechanical plumbing system**. By replacing 0-dimensional point particles and empty space with **3D Fluid Knots** and a high-density **Acoustic Superfluid**, the anomalies of both quantum mechanics and general relativity resolve themselves. We don't need "new physics"; we just need to fix the leaks.

## ⚙️ Core Architecture (The Axioms)

To run this universe, you only need to accept two foundational variables:

1.  **The Absolute Medium:** Space is not empty. It is a physical, high-density ($\rho \approx 10^{11} \text{ kg/m}^3$) acoustic superfluid. It exists under a constant, crushing baseline static pressure ($P_0$).
2.  **The Standard Pipe (The Rankine Vortex):** There are no "fundamental particles." Matter is simply the **Standard Universal Pipe**—a fluid vortex tube—tied into various 3D topological knots. To survive the absolute medium without acoustic shearing, this pipe operates as a textbook **Rankine Vortex**: it features a microscopic "redline" core spinning at the acoustic limit ($c$), surrounded by a smooth irrotational envelope where the fluid velocity drops off proportionally to the radius ($v \propto 1/r$).

From these two rules, all "forces" emerge as simple plumbing consequences:

* **Mass (Dual-State):**
    * *Resting:* **Hydrostatic Displacement** ($\Delta V$). The energy of the "hole" in the high-pressure medium.
    * *Inertial:* **Hydrodynamic Drag** ($A_{eff}$). The resistance of the knot's viscous boundary layer as it is pushed through the $10^{11}$ ocean.
* **Charge:** **Volumetric Flow Rate** ($Q$). The integrated amount of fluid the "pump" moves per second.
* **Gravity:** The **Inward Pressure Gradient** (Bernoulli Sink). The extreme $c$-velocity core creates an absolute static pressure minimum, and the $1/r$ velocity envelope mathematically generates Newton's exact $1/r^2$ gravitational drop-off.
* **Magnetism:** The **Torsional Wake**. The swirling current created as a "pump" (charge) moves through the medium.
