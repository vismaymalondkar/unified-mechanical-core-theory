## 3. Frequency ($f$): The Length of the Pipe
If the width of the pipe is a universal constant, how do different particles have different masses? 

In the UPT, mass is the hydrodynamic drag created by the knot. To create more drag, the knot must displace more fluid. If the width cannot change, the only way to increase the volume of the knot is to **lengthen the pipe**.
