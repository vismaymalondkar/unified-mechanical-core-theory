In the UPT, the fundamental law of Energy is the total length of the physical wire ($E \propto L_{pipe}$). How does a photon scale its energy (from a weak Radio wave to a deadly Gamma ray) if it is just a moving spring?

* **The Wire Length ($L_{pipe}$):** If you take a 1-meter long physical space, a tightly coiled Gamma-ray spring will contain miles of compressed physical wire inside that 1-meter span. A loosely coiled Radio-wave spring will contain only a few inches of wire in that same span.
