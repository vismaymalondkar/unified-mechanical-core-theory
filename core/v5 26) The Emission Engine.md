# **Universal Plumbing Theory: The Emission Engine**

In the UPT, a photon is not propelled by a magical external force. Its frequency and its velocity are strictly derived from the internal elastic tension and rotational momentum of the severed loop. 

When a closed geometric knot ($0_1$ donut) is structurally severed, the emission follows a strict three-step kinematic sequence:

**1. The Symmetrical Snap (Releasing the Strain)**
When the loop is cut, the immense geometric tension (Isentropic Acoustic Strain / Borrowed Length) is released at the point of the sever. Acting like a highly strained rubber band, the two severed ends violently whip away from the cut point, traveling backward along the circumference. 

**2. The Anti-Node Crush (The Ignition of Frequency)**
The two whipping ends crash into each other exactly 180 degrees from the cut (the anti-node). 
* At this exact moment, the particle has no linear velocity, and therefore no Pilot Wave. The kinetic collision provides the initial physical hammer required to crush the spring. 
* The longer the severed wire, the greater the compressible strain, and the more violent the crash. A massive wire (Gamma Ray) kinetically crushes itself into an ultra-dense, high-frequency block, while a tiny wire (Radio Wave) gently bunches into a loose, low-frequency wave.

**3. Linear Translation (The Aerodynamic Hand-Off)**
Prior to being severed, the surface of the closed vortex ring was spinning (poloidal rotation) at the fluid's acoustic speed limit ($c$). 
* When the symmetrical crash straightens the circular wire into a linear spear, Conservation of Angular Momentum strictly dictates that the rotational kinetic energy must be preserved.
* The $c$ rotational velocity is instantly unrolled and translated into a $c$ linear velocity vector. The photon shrapnel stops spinning in a circle and begins flying in a straight line.
* **The Ramjet Ignition:** The newly exposed open ends of the wire instantly begin ramming the resting ocean fluid into the core, establishing the internal Choked Flow streamline ($P^*$).
* **The Aerodynamic Lock:** The instant the photon hits Mach 1, its rotating coils generate the frontal shock boundary (The Pilot Wave). The aerodynamic drag ($F_{shock}$) of this newly formed wall perfectly "catches" the initial kinetic compression of the spring, providing the exact, sustained backward pressure required to hold the photon at that frequency permanently as it crosses the universe.
