# **Universal Plumbing Theory: The Pilot Wave Pressure Equation**

**Core Thesis:** Mainstream physics dictates that high-frequency photons carry more energy ($E=hf$), but offers no mechanical reason why. By deriving the exact mass flow rate of the photon's continuous fluid displacement, we can calculate the absolute force of the Pilot Wave and mechanically decode the linearity of Planck's equation.

---

## **1. The Acoustic Mass Flow Rate ($\dot{m}$)**
As the open photon spring flies through the resting ocean at $c$, its corrugated helical coils rotate. Every single $360^\circ$ rotation acts as a physical piston, performing a **transverse displacement** of a strictly quantified volume of fluid ($V_{strike}$) outward from the $r_{pipe}$ boundary.

Because the resting ocean has a constant density ($\rho_0 = 1.129 \times 10^{11} \text{ kg/m}^3$), every rotation displaces a highly specific mass of fluid:
$$\text{Mass per rotation} = \rho_0 \cdot V_{strike}$$

The photon is rotating at its Frequency ($f$), meaning it completes $f$ rotations per second. Therefore, the total fluid mass the photon displaces per second (The Mass Flow Rate, $\dot{m}$) is:
$$\dot{m} = \rho_0 \cdot V_{strike} \cdot f$$

---

## **2. The Mach 1 Momentum Transfer (The Shock Force)**
If the photon were standing still, this **transverse displacement** would simply radiate away as a standard wake. But the photon is flying at exactly the speed of sound ($c$). 

Because the wave cannot outrun the spring, $100\%$ of this displaced mass flow ($\dot{m}$) stacks up exactly at the front edge of the particle, violently compressing into a **Longitudinal Shock Wave (The Pilot Wave)**. 

According to Newton's Second Law, Force is the transfer of momentum over time ($F = \dot{m} \cdot \Delta v$). The shock wave is composed of the fluid being violently accelerated to the acoustic limit ($c$) to get out of the way. Therefore, the aerodynamic force ($F_{shock}$) pushing backward against the front of the photon is:
$$F_{shock} = \dot{m} \cdot c$$

Substitute our strictly derived fluid mass flow rate into the equation:
$$F_{shock} = (\rho_0 \cdot V_{strike} \cdot f) \cdot c$$

Rearrange the variables to isolate Frequency:
$$\mathbf{F_{shock} = (\rho_0 \cdot V_{strike} \cdot c) \cdot f}$$

## **3. The Hydrodynamic Planck Constant**
Look closely at the equation we just derived: $F_{shock} = (\rho_0 \cdot V_{strike} \cdot c) \cdot f$. 

Everything inside the parentheses is an absolute, invariant physical constant of the standard UPT fluid engine:
* $\rho_0$ is the constant density of the universe.
* $c$ is the constant speed of sound.
* $V_{strike}$ is the constant geometric fluid volume displaced by one rotation of the standard $r_{pipe}$ Toroidal wire. 

Let us bundle these three fluid constants into a single universal coefficient of displacement ($h_{hydro}$):
$$h_{hydro} = \rho_0 \cdot V_{strike} \cdot c$$

Substitute this back into the shock wave equation, and the architecture becomes flawless:
$$\mathbf{F_{shock} = h_{hydro} \cdot f}$$

The equation $\text{Energy} = h \cdot f$ is not a quantum mystery. It is the exact aerodynamic formula for Mach 1 fluid drag.
