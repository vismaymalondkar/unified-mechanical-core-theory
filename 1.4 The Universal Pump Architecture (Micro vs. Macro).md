# Unified Mechanical Core Theory: The Universal Pump Architecture (Micro vs. Macro)

## 1. The Core Geometry: The Two-Way Toroidal Pump
A Toroidal Vortex (wave-knot) cannot exist as a static object or a one-way drain. It is a continuous hydrodynamic engine submerged in the universal fluid medium. To maintain its structure, it must continuously cycle fluid.
* **The Intake (Equatorial Pull):** The rotational deficit pulls the surrounding high-pressure fluid inward from 360 degrees toward the center/equator. 
* **The Exhaust (Polar Jets):** The fluid is routed through the central axis (the "donut hole") and violently expelled out the North and South poles to prevent the system from tangling or over-pressurizing.

## 2. The Microscopic Execution: The Electron
At the quantum scale, this pump operates on the smallest possible unit of fluid circulation (spinning at $c$).

* **The Intake (Gravity/Mass):** The spinning core creates a tiny, localized pressure drop. The universal medium presses inward to crush it. We measure this inward static pressure as the particle's microscopic Gravity and resting Mass.
* **The Exhaust (Magnetic Dipole):** The fluid cycling through the central axis and spraying out the poles creates a localized, directional cross-current. We measure this microscopic polar exhaust as the particle's Intrinsic Magnetic Field (North/South poles).

## 3. The Macroscopic Execution: The Active Black Hole (Quasar)
When trillions of particles are crushed together by a dying star, they merge into a single Mega-Vortex. The "spherical average" of the star is destroyed, exposing the raw, scaled-up Toroidal geometry of a single wave-knot.

* **The Intake (The Accretion Disk):** The pressure deficit is now massive. It pulls in the universal medium and physical matter with devastating force. This matter swirls violently around the equator, forming a glowing, superheated Accretion Disk. This is the macroscopic equivalent of particle gravity.
* **The Exhaust (Relativistic Jets):** The Black Hole does not just consume. The fluid and matter routed into the central axis are accelerated by immense torsion and blasted out of the North and South poles at 99% the speed of light. These Astrophysical Jets can span millions of lightyears. This is the macroscopic equivalent of a magnetic dipole.
