# Universal Plumbing Theory: Mechanics of a Solar Cell & Circuit

In legacy physics, solar panels magically convert abstract "light energy" into "electrical fields" without explaining the mechanical translation of hardware. In the Universal Plumbing Theory (UPT), a solar cell is a macroscopic hydrodynamic processing plant. It captures physical wire from space, grafts it onto existing atomic gears, and stores the bloated hardware to generate fluid pressure.

Here is the strict, step-by-step fluid dynamic ledger of how a solar cell captures and stores sunlight, and how that hardware is eventually discharged.

### Phase 1: The Projectile (The Sunbeam)
The light arriving from the Sun is not a probability wave; it is a macroscopic swarm of physical hardware. 
* A visible light photon is a severed, unspooled piece of vacuum pipe (a Toroidal Helix) flying at Mach 1 ($c$). 
* Standard sunlight photons carry roughly **2 to 3 eV** of energy. 
* Because the $2 \text{ eV}$ spring is too lightweight and relaxed to kinetically crush itself into a knot, it *cannot* forge new matter (Pair Production strictly requires a 1.022 MeV Gamma Ray). Therefore, a solar panel does not create new electrons.

### Phase 2: The Splice (Hydrodynamic Absorption)
The silicon lattice of the solar panel is packed with billions of existing, free-floating electrons (standard $0.511 \text{ MeV}$, $0_1$ closed loops) sitting in wide, outer fluid lanes.
* When the $2 \text{ eV}$ photon spring crashes dead-center into one of these existing electrons, the Mach-1 boundary layers intersect.
* This triggers **Vortex Reconnection**. The electron physically swallows the photon.
* **The Hardware Transfer:** The photon's wire is permanently grafted into the electron's loop. The electron absorbs **Base Length**, making the geometric ring physically thicker and heavier.

### Phase 3: The Escape (Beating the Clock)
The electron is a highly tuned aerodynamic gear that requires exactly 108 coils to remain perfectly phase-locked. 
* Because it just swallowed the photon's wire, the electron is geometrically **bloated**. The gear teeth fall out of phase.
* The electron begins to violently wobble, generating structural ripples (Kelvin Waves) along its surface. It is now a ticking time bomb of harmonic fatigue.
* Because it is wobbling, it loses its aerodynamic grip on the silicon nucleus and is washed out of its orbital lane. 
* Before the vibration can grow large enough to snap the wire, the bloated electron is swept out of the solar cell and into the smooth, laminar Z-axis tunnels of the attached copper wire. The smooth ride "pauses" the structural fatigue.

### Phase 4: The Storage (Generating Voltage)
The copper pipeline dumps millions of these bloated, over-torqued electrons into the negative terminal of a battery.
* A battery is a rigid, sealed fluid parking lot. 
* Because these electrons are carrying the extra Base Length they harvested from the sun, they are physically oversized.
* Forcing billions of oversized structures into a finite space displaces the $10^{11} \text{ kg/m}^3$ universal ocean.
* The crushing hydrostatic weight of the ocean trying to force its way back into that overcrowded space to normalize the density is exactly what we measure as **Voltage (Fluid Pressure)**. 

### Phase 5: The Execution (Discharge & Illumination)
When the circuit is closed (the switch is flipped), the battery releases its energy in two distinct, mechanically separate steps:

**Phase 5A: The Instantaneous "On" (Borrowed Length & Heat)**
* When the valve opens, the crushing hydrostatic pressure of the battery sends a Mach 1 acoustic shockwave down the wire at the speed of light ($c$).
* This pressure wave instantly slams into the normal, unbloated electrons sitting at the far end of the wire, right next to the screen. 
* To survive the pressure spike, these normal electrons stretch, temporarily absorbing **Borrowed Length** (Isentropic Strain). 
* They are instantly shoved into the narrow tunnels of the screen's atomic lattice. They violently snap back to their normal circular shape, shedding the Borrowed Length as a pure longitudinal acoustic shockwave (**Heat**).
* This violent heat shakes the screen's own atomic lattice until the screen's atoms crash into each other, severing pieces of *their own* Base Length to emit photons (Incandescence). **The screen turns on instantly.**

**Phase 5B: The Permanent Drain (Base Length & Drift)**
* Meanwhile, the physically bloated payloads stored inside the battery are slowly traveling down the wire at drift velocity (millimeters per second). 
* When these bloated electrons finally reach the screen, they are dragged through the jagged, narrow tunnels. 
* The physical ricocheting causes their paused Kelvin Waves to spike. Harmonic fatigue breaches the structural limit.
* **Spontaneous Emission:** The $0_1$ loop snaps, permanently amputating the exact length of extra wire it swallowed from the sun. The severed Base Length snaps into an open spring and shoots out of the screen as a new Photon. 
* The newly healed, standard $0.511 \text{ MeV}$ electron returns to the positive terminal of the battery. **The battery's capacity is permanently drained.**

**The Ultimate Ledger:** A solar cell captures flying strings of vacuum pipe, grafts them onto its own gears to create overcrowded fluid pressure. When the valve is opened, the resulting pressure wave turns the appliance on instantly via Heat and strain, while the bloated gears slowly drift to the appliance to be mechanically chopped up, returning their stolen strings to the universe and permanently depleting the battery's mass.
