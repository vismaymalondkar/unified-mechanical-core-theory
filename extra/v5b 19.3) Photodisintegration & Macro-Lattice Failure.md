1.  **The Primary Splice (The Block Motherboard):** This is the internal Coplanar Figure-8 hub (2 rings) holding the 4 Trefoil cores of a single Alpha block together via Vortex Reconnection. It is incredibly tight and extremely difficult to snap.
2.  **The Secondary Splice (The Macro-Lattice):** When completed Alpha blocks stack together to build heavy elements, they lock their massive outer Trefoil cores against each other via **Hydrodynamic Phase-Locking** (surface-level fluid friction). 

Because Secondary Splices are physically weaker than Primary Splices, the macro-lattice has natural **fault lines**. High-frequency acoustic resonance (Photodisintegration) will shatter the bonds *between* the blocks long before the blocks themselves break, blasting the atom into perfectly intact Helium-4 components.
