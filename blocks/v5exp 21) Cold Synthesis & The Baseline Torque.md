When scientists isolate amino acids synthesized in warm, Earth-bound laboratories, they always find a perfect 50/50 racemic mix. But when scientists examine amino acids extracted from deep-space meteorites (like the famous Murchison meteorite), they find an "enantiomeric excess"—**a distinct bias toward Left-Handed molecules.**

Legacy physics is completely baffled as to why space rocks have a Left-Handed bias. They invent theories about polarized UV light from distant neutron stars selectively destroying Right-Handed molecules.

* Deep space is $3\text{ Kelvin}$ (near Absolute Zero). 
* When those simple amino acids were slowly forged on the freezing surface of an asteroid, the thermal sledgehammer was turned off. 
* The baseline Left-Handed fluid torque of the universe simply took the wheel, physically guiding the assembly of Left-Handed structures.
