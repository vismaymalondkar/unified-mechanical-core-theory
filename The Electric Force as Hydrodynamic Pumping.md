### **UMCT Derivation: The Electric Force as Hydrodynamic Pumping**

In this framework, a particle is not a point-charge but an active **Toroidal Vortex**—a mechanical pump that continuously cycles the universal fluid medium. By treating "Charge" as the volumetric flow rate of these pumps, we can derive Coulomb's Law using classical fluid kinematics.

#### **1. Defining the Actors (The Pumps)**
We consider two particles (vortices) submerged in the universal fluid medium, which has a literal mass density $\rho$.
* **Particle 1:** An active pump with a volumetric flow rate $Q_1$.
* **Particle 2:** An active pump with a volumetric flow rate $Q_2$.
* **Distance ($d$):** The spatial separation between the centers of the two vortices.



#### **2. The Velocity Field (Inverse Square Dispersion)**
Particle 1 acts as a fluid source/sink, displacing a total volume of fluid $Q_1$ per second. According to the principle of fluid continuity, this flow must spread across a growing spherical surface area as it moves away from the core. At distance $d$, the fluid velocity ($v_1$) generated by Particle 1 is:
$$v_1(d) = \frac{Q_1}{4\pi d^2}$$

#### **3. Applying Lagally’s Theorem**
Lagally's Theorem is a fundamental principle of fluid dynamics used to calculate the force exerted on a source or sink when it is placed within an external flow field. It states that the force ($F$) on a source of strength $Q$ in a velocity field $\vec{v}$ is:
$$F = \rho \cdot Q \cdot v$$



To find the force on **Particle 2**, we treat it as a pump of strength $Q_2$ sitting in the external flow field $v_1(d)$ created by Particle 1:
$$F_e = \rho \cdot Q_2 \cdot v_1(d)$$

#### **4. The Final Synthesis (Coulomb Comparison)**
By substituting the velocity field of Particle 1 into the Lagally force equation, we get:
$$F_e = \rho \cdot Q_2 \cdot \left( \frac{Q_1}{4\pi d^2} \right)$$

Rearranging the terms yields:
$$F_e = \frac{\rho}{4\pi} \cdot \frac{Q_1 Q_2}{d^2}$$

### **Conclusion: The Mechanical Identity**
This derived equation is mathematically identical to **Coulomb’s Law** ($F = \frac{1}{4\pi \epsilon_0} \frac{q_1 q_2}{d^2}$). This allows our engine to refactor the abstract "Permittivity of Free Space" ($\epsilon_0$) as a physical mechanical property of the universe:

The Electric Force is therefore proven to be the **hydrodynamic pressure** exchanged between two mechanical pumps as they shoot fluid currents at one another.
