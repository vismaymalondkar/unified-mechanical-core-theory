# **Universal Plumbing Theory: The Helical Length Lock ($E=hf$)**

**Core Thesis:** In the UPT, the Mass of a particle is strictly the total length of its vortex wire ($L_{pipe}$). When a particle annihilates into a Photon, that exact length of wire must be conserved as an Open Spring. We must mathematically prove how conserving a fixed wire length naturally dictates the discrete quantization of light ($E=hf$).

---

## **1. The Three Variables of a Photon Spring**
A photon is a 3D helix. To mathematically define it, we must separate the *Wire* from the *Pulse*. 
1. **Total Wire Length ($L_{pipe}$):** The absolute, conserved length of the thread. (Fixed by the parent particle).
2. **Wavelength ($\lambda$):** The linear distance between one coil and the next. (Fixed by the structural RPMs of the parent particle). 
3. **Number of Coils ($N$):** The total number of $360^\circ$ turns the spring makes from front to back.

---

## **2. The Pythagorean Lock**
If you take a single $360^\circ$ coil of a spring and unroll it flat, it forms a perfect right triangle:
* The **Base** is the circumference of the spiral ($2\pi R$).
* The **Height** is the Wavelength ($\lambda$).
* The **Hypotenuse** is the length of physical wire required to make that single coil ($l_{coil}$).

By the Pythagorean theorem, the wire length for one single coil is locked:
$$l_{coil} = \sqrt{(2\pi R)^2 + \lambda^2}$$

To find the Total Wire Length of the entire photon, you simply multiply the length of one coil by the total Number of Coils ($N$):
$$L_{pipe} = N \cdot \sqrt{(2\pi R)^2 + \lambda^2}$$

---

## **3. Deriving Frequency (Why Coils Multiply)**
This simple geometric equation is the mechanical engine behind Planck's $E=hf$. 

If an Electron annihilates, its Total Wire Length ($L_{pipe}$) is a permanently fixed number. Because the wire thickness is locked, the radius of the spiral ($R$) is also fixed. 

Therefore, if the RPMs of the snapping electron dictate a tight, high-tension spring (a very small $\lambda$), the hypotenuse ($l_{coil}$) shrinks. 
* **The Lock:** Because the Total Wire Length ($L_{pipe}$) cannot change, if $l_{coil}$ shrinks, the universe is mathematically forced to increase the **Number of Coils ($N$)** to use up the remaining wire! 

A tight spring (Gamma Ray) contains thousands of densely packed coils. A loose spring (Radio Wave) contains only a few stretched-out coils. They are made of the exact same amount of wire, but their geometries dictate entirely different coil counts.

---

## **4. The Mechanical Translation of Planck ($h$)**
We can now map this directly to classical Quantum Mechanics. 
* **Planck's Constant ($h$):** In the UPT, $h$ is the kinematic action (fluid displacement) of exactly **One Single Coil** ($l_{coil}$) striking a target. It is the fundamental quantum of action. 
* **Frequency ($f$):** Frequency is simply the Total Number of Coils ($N$) striking the target per second. 
* **Total Energy ($E$):** $$E = h \cdot f$$

**Conclusion:** The energy of a photon scales linearly with its frequency because Frequency is simply a count of how many coils are in the spring. A tighter spring mathematically forces the wire to loop more times ($N$ increases). Because every single loop delivers exactly $h$ amount of fluid kinetic energy, more loops equals a more devastating impact. $E=hf$ is perfectly conserved by the Pythagorean theorem of a fixed-length wire.
