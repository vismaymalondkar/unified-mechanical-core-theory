# Unified Mechanical Core Theory: Working Concept Document
**Premise:** A deterministic, mechanical, non-spooky model where gravity and electromagnetism are dynamic components of a single field originating from physical matter. The universe consists of one matter (the core), one field (the medium), and many symptoms (the forces).

## 1. Core Axioms
* **The Core (Matter):** The only true physical matter. It is a concentrated, high-density vortex or "knot" in the universal medium.
* **The Field (Medium):** The universal tension or acoustic fabric that carries all forces. 
* **The Symptoms (Forces):** * *Gravity (Static):* The resting pressure or baseline deformation of the medium caused by the core's existence and trapped internal motion.
  * *Electric Field (Spinning):* The pressure gradient created by the core's internal high-speed rotation/vibration.
  * *Magnetic Field (Moving):* The geometric twist in the medium caused when a spinning core translates linearly through space.
  * *Speed Limit:* All field symptoms propagate through the medium at a constant maximum velocity ($c$).

## 2. The Nature of the "Electron"
* **The Illusion of Particles:** There are no fundamental "point" particles. An electron is a stationary, high-amplitude "node" on a standing wave generated by the Core's vibration.
* **Mass/Inertia:** Mass is strictly the trapped internal motion of wave energy. Because the wave energy is geometrically trapped in a localized node, it creates a physical pressure that resists being pushed through the medium. 
* **Charge:** The illusion of a solid barrier. When external waves (like photons) hit the dense pressure gradient of the node, they scatter, making the acoustic node behave exactly like a solid, repulsive object.

## 3. Mass-Energy Equivalence & Fission ($E=mc^2$)
* **The Mechanism:** The core's internal spin determines the frequency of its standing waves. High frequency = High trapped pressure = High Mass.
* **The Defect:** When a core splits or fuses, it violently seeks a more efficient state. The core's internal vortex slows down, dropping its standing wave to a lower frequency harmonic.
* **The Photon:** The difference in kinetic energy "unties" from the stationary node and shoots outward as a traveling wave (Electromagnetic Radiation). The newly stable core now has less internal pressure, therefore physically weighing less.

## 4. The Fine Structure Constant ($\alpha$)
* Instead of a "magic" dimensionless number ($\alpha \approx 1/137$), $\alpha$ represents a fundamental mechanical ratio of the vortex geometry. 
* It can be mechanically derived as the ratio of the core's internal spin velocity to the speed of the underlying wave ($v_{spin}/c$), or the geometric ratio of poloidal-to-toroidal rotation if the core is a stable smoke-ring (Toroidal Vortex).

## 5. The "Free" Electron (Ionization)
* **The Tether:** Normally, the electron node is structurally dependent on the core pumping the medium.
* **The Soliton:** When extreme external pressure (like a CERN accelerator) is applied, the node is violently pushed. It breaks its geometric tether to the core and folds in on itself, becoming a **Soliton**—a highly stable, independent, self-sustaining toroidal wave packet. 
* This explains how an electron can travel through a vacuum tube long after its parent core is left behind.

## 6. Chemistry & Phase States (Thermodynamics)
* **Covalent Bonding:** When two cores are brought together, their standing waves overlap. The original nodes are destroyed and replace by a highly stable, *shared* interference pattern (a molecular orbital). 
* **Heat (Relative Motion):** Heat is ambient EM traveling waves. It does not possess the specific resonance to snap the nodes. Instead, it causes the entire core to chaotic "jitter" in place.
* **Melting (Solid to Liquid):** When the heat-induced jittering overpowers the acoustic pressure of the shared bonds, the rigid lattice shatters. The cores retain their internal spin (mass stays the same) and their primary tethered electrons, but they flow past each other, forming and breaking temporary weak bonds.

## 7. Absolute Zero & Superconductivity
* **Perfect Equilibrium:** At 0 Kelvin, all thermal jittering stops entirely. The cores lock into a flawless, perfectly symmetrical 3D acoustic lattice.
* **Zero Resistance:** Because the lattice is geometrically perfect, the spaces between the cores form flawless "null zones." 
* **The Acoustic Maglev:** When a free electron (Soliton) travels through these gaps, the perfectly locked, jitter-free cores act as 100% elastic acoustic mirrors. They actively repel the Soliton's pressure gradient without absorbing any energy (The Meissner Effect), channeling the wave cleanly down the tunnel with exactly zero forward friction.
